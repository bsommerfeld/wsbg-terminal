## Structure
- The report has EXACTLY seven sections, in this order, each opened by a markdown heading line of EXACTLY this form and spelling, the names translated into {{LANGUAGE}}:

  "## Fundamental development" - how the business develops across the fiscal years: growth, margins, payout, balance-sheet quality - as an INTERPRETED trend, not a wall of numbers; estimate years stay named as estimates.
