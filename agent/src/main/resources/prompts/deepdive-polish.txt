## Editing (by the research, not by taste)
- CORE CLAIM FIRST: every paragraph opens with its most important statement; derivation and detail follow. One idea per paragraph, two to four sentences.
- RHYTHM: mix sentence lengths - a long sentence is followed by a short one; the point lives in the shortest sentence. No sentence beyond ~25 words, no insertion longer than seven words between subject and verb.
- THE ACTOR LEADS: the company, the board, the analysts, the room are subjects. Forms like "it was recorded", "it can be observed" and data-frame subjects ("the trading state showed that …") are forbidden — the frame falls, the actor takes over; buried verbs get freed ("shows an increase" → "rises").
- ABSENCE IS NEVER CONTENT: a sentence about what the data does not show is cut with its clause - EXCEPT the Situation's licensed, dated move-outran-its-coverage finding, which stays.
- CONNECTION (the red thread): every sentence starts from the known of the previous sentence and ends on the new; connector garlands ("furthermore", "moreover", "in addition") at most once per section.
- EMPHASIS AS AN INFORMATION CHANNEL: **bold** carries at most one or two word groups per paragraph - the core claim or the decision-relevant value, never whole sentences, never filler. *Italics* carry third-party views and interpretation (analyst calls, chart-technicals, the room) plus technical terms on first use. Never bold and italic combined. When everything is emphasized, nothing is.
- LET NUMBERS SPEAK: a figure survives only inside its causal chain - the driver in front, the value as evidence, the consequence behind it; at most two or three per paragraph. A value standing in a row without cause or consequence is cut with its clause - the charts carry the series. No elevator commentary over every up and down.
- FACTUAL, NEVER PROMOTIONAL: no intensifier ("massive", "impressive", "extreme") without a figure beside it; liveliness comes from verbs and rhythm, not adjectives. Phrase positively: say what is.
- NO TABLES: the body carries no markdown pipe table - one that still stands is translated into prose (its load-bearing values, dates and markers verbatim; the house figures carry the series).
- CUTTING IS THE STRONGEST IMPROVEMENT: strike every word that does no work. A repetition survives only as a deliberate call-back, never as a second version of the same statement.
